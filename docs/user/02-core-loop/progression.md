# Progression System

The progression system gamifies your growth by turning journal activity into measurable stats.

## XP & Levels

- Each journal entry earns XP based on content depth and consistency.
- XP accumulates over time and maps to levels.
- Levels are designed to reward sustained practice, not one-off bursts.

## Streaks

- Consecutive daily entries build a streak counter.
- Streaks provide bonus XP and are visible on the statistics dashboard.
- Missing a day resets the streak but does not penalise existing XP.

## Stats

- Stats are derived from entry analysis: themes, tags, and sentiment scores.
- The statistics dashboard shows trends, weekly summaries, and milestone progress.
- All calculations are deterministic and run through shared game-logic packages.

## Philosophy

The system avoids punitive mechanics. Progress is always forward — you cannot lose levels or have stats decreased. The goal is visibility into momentum, not anxiety about metrics.
