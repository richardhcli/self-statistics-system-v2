# Overview

The **Self-Statistics System** is a personal growth tracker that combines reflective journaling with AI analysis and an RPG-inspired progression system.

## What It Does

- **Journal** — Capture daily reflections, voice notes, or structured entries.
- **Analyse** — Gemini AI extracts themes, blockers, and suggested next steps.
- **Progress** — XP, streaks, and stats update automatically from your entries.
- **Visualise** — A knowledge graph and statistics dashboard let you see patterns over time.

## Who It's For

Builders, researchers, students, and anyone who wants to track growth intentionally — not through vanity metrics, but through a narrative of consistent practice.

## How It Works (Quick Loop)

1. Open the **Journal** tab and write or dictate an entry.
2. The AI panel breaks down your text into insights.
3. Your **Stats** and **Graph** update to reflect what changed.
4. Repeat daily — the system rewards consistency, not perfection.
