# Overview

The **Self-Statistics System** is a self-motivation system inspired by gaming systems. It maintains a personal "status screen" — a quantifiable view of your own progress over time — sourcing reflection and motivation.

## What It Does

- **Capture** — Voice, quick notes, or structured journal entries keep your daily signals in one place.
- **Extract** — Gemini AI extracts actions, skills, and characteristics from your entries and maps them onto a Characteristic DAG (CDAG).
- **Progress** — Actions and time convert into XP that feed-forwards through the CDAG, updating every relevant node automatically.
- **Visualise** — A statistics dashboard and the CDAG graph let you see your current state, levels, and effort over time.

## Who It's For

Anyone who wants a human-centric value tracker — gamify your growth and quantify your progress rather than relying on vanity metrics.

## How It Works (Quick Loop)

1. **Input** — Enter moment-to-moment small gains throughout your day.
2. **Feedback** — View instantaneous quantified progress for each entry.
3. **Accumulate** — Each progression is stored and compounded, preparing for analysis.
4. **Self Statistics** — View your holistic current state in the status window.
5. **Cross-Compare** — See which path you are on, how far you have gone, and what it takes to reach the next level.
